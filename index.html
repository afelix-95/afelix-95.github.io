About Me

Hello! I'm Alison Felix, currently residing in the vibrant city of Porto, Portugal. My journey in the realm of technology started with a deep-rooted interest in computers from an early age. However, when it came time for formal education, I ventured into the world of materials science, specializing in nanomaterials.

Engaging in scientific research was indeed fascinating, but the inherent challenges of experimental science, where it takes a considerable amount of time to make progress, prompted me to seek a different kind of thrill.

In the pursuit of more immediate and tangible results, I decided to pivot my career toward software development. At its core, writing software shares common ground with conducting research. The analytical approach, problem-solving methodology, and experimentation remain consistent.

Beyond the professional realm, I find joy in video games, exploring the great outdoors through hiking, and savoring the diverse offerings of various cuisines. These activities provide the perfect balance to my passion for technology and keep life exciting and multifaceted.

Another interesting aspect of my life is the diverse set of countries I've called home - from Brazil and the United States to France, Poland, Italy, and now Portugal. Each place has added its unique flavor to my experiences, shaping both my personal and professional journey.

Let's connect and explore the fascinating intersection of science, technology, and creativity!
